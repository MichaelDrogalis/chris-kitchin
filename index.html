    Simple Sustainable Fitness

    Fitness matters, but why is it so tough to keep a program going? Sometimes it just doesn't stick. I think a lot of our fitness gets derailed when we complicate things and force ourselves into attaining goals that contradict our lifestyles.

    When I work with my clients, I strive to understand their needs, professional responsibilities and personal commitments. Taking the whole person into account means I can craft better fitness plans that work with and not against a person's everyday life. All of this happens at home, making my coaching not just convenient but also effective longterm. Let's simplify your fitness!

    Home Is Where The Fitness Is

    Quality fitness doesn't have to come from a gym. It doesn't require expensive equipment either. No, quality fitness is determined by simple, precise movements and consistent application. I work with all my clients from their homes.

    Why? The real work begins when I leave. People are more likely to maintain their fitness with a trainer beside them, coaching them through the exercises. That's what I love to do, but it's also important for my clients to do some solo homework. Having a plan that fits the person and the person's space/equipment restrictions means a greater likelihoood he or she will keep up the work - and look forward to doing the work in the first place.
